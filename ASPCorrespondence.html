Alonso S. Perales Correspondence

This map visualizes a selection of correspondence received by the Mexican American civil rights leader, Alonso S. Perales (1898-1960). Perales was a co-founder of the League of United Latin American Citizens (LULAC) and one of the most prominent US civil rights leaders of the twentieth century.

How to Browse

Mouse over a point on the map and click to view information about the envelop related to this point. A pop-up box will display the item metadata (photograph identifier, link to image, title, name of sender, and name of receiver). Click on the map and then use mouse scroll function to zoom in and out of the map.

How to Cite this Project

This digital project is a product of decades of collaboration in its two forms (print bibliography and digital map) at Recovering the US Hispanic Literary Heritage. To cite the digital project, we suggest the following citation:

Alonso S. Perales Correspondence. Recovering the US Hispanic Literary Heritage. https://recoveryapp.github.io/ASPCorrespondence.html. Accessed [DATE].
